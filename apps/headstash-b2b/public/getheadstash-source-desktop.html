Is there really no rev share?
No rev share, no take-rate, no percentage of redemption value. Flat monthly platform fee. You keep 100% of the merch revenue the program drives.

Early access rates go month-to-month at the locked $1,500 rate through month 24. Standard contracts renew annually with market-rate pricing.
Who pays for the merch that gets redeemed?
You do - it's your inventory out of your Shopify. Same merch economics as your IG store, just moving to a qualified, receipt-verified audience instead of random traffic.
