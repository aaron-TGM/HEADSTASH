FAQ

Quick answers.

HOW MUCH DOES IT COST?
Free. The cost is receipts, not cash. No subscription, no fees, no premium tier.
WHAT DO I ACTUALLY GET?
Depends on the brand. Some send merch when you hit a spend threshold. Some send invites to drops or events. Some surprise you with limited stuff that is not even on their store. Each brand decides what counts and what they send. You see what is available in your stash from day one.
IS MY PURCHASE HISTORY PRIVATE?
Yes. Each brand sees only the SKUs from their own products in your receipts. Nobody else can see what you bought, where, or when. Your data is not sold, is not shared with employers or insurers, and you can delete your account and everything in it anytime.
WHAT IF I FORGET TO UPLOAD?
You have fourteen days from the date on the receipt. After that, the receipt expires and will not earn anything. We will text reminders if you go a few days without uploading after a known purchase, so you do not lose what you already earned.
WHAT HAPPENS WHEN I SIGN UP?
You get a six-digit code by text. Enter it. Your stash is empty until you upload your first receipt - usually that is where the first welcome reward fires. No app store download. Add Headstash to your home screen and it works like an app.
CAN I USE THIS FOR DELIVERY ORDERS?
Yes. Forward the order confirmation email to receipts@joinheadstash.com. Same outcome - we identify the brand SKUs and credit your stash automatically.
