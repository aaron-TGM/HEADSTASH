What brands can reward you for

Things you already do.

Buying.
Every receipt with a participating SKU adds qualifying dollars to that brand's stash.
Repeating.
Hit a brand's spend or receipt milestone and a named item appears in your stash.
Trying.
Your first receipt with a new brand can trigger a welcome item.
Exploring.
Some brands reward buying their SKUs across multiple dispensaries - something Headstash can uniquely track.
Putting people on.
Refer a friend to a brand you already shop. When their first receipt lands, you both unlock something.

FAQ

Quick answers.

How much does it cost?
Free. The cost is receipts, not cash.
Does Headstash sell anything?
No. Brands run their own rewards. Headstash connects your receipt to them.
Do receipts expire?
Fourteen days from purchase. Don't sit on them.
Is this a discount program?
No. Brands reward you with merch, drops, or access - not generic coupons.
Can I share receipts with a friend?
One receipt, one account. We dedupe.
