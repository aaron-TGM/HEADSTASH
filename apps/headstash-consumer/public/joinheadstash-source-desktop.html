What brands can reward you for

Things you already do.

Buying.
Every receipt containing a participating brand's SKU adds qualifying dollars to that brand's stash.
Repeating.
Hit a brand's spend or receipt milestone in a rolling window. A real named item shows up in your stash.
Trying.
First receipt with a new brand usually triggers a welcome item. Most brands say hello with something.
Exploring.
Some brands reward buying their SKUs across multiple dispensaries. The kind of thing only Headstash can see.
Putting people on.
Refer a head to a brand you already shop. When their first receipt lands, you both get something.

FAQ

Quick answers.

How much does it cost?
Free. Pay with receipts.
Does Headstash sell anything?
No. Each brand you shop runs their own rewards. Headstash just connects your receipt to them.
Do receipts expire?
Fourteen days from purchase. Don't sit on them.
Is this a discount program?
No. Brands reward you with their merch, drops, or access. Never coupons, never percent-off.
Can I share receipts with a friend?
One receipt, one account. We dedupe.
